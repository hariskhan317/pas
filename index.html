Recent floods have devastated communities across Pakistan—destroying homes, livelihoods, and critical infrastructure. Your donation helps deliver food, clean water, medical supplies, and emergency shelter through verified on-ground partners.

How funds are used

Emergency Aid

Food kits, clean water, blankets, and tents for displaced families.

Medical Support

First-aid kits, essential medicines, and clinic support.

Rebuilding

Repairing homes, restoring schools, and livelihood recovery.

About Us

The PAK American Society is a volunteer‑led, non‑political community group. Our mission is to celebrate culture, support families, and empower youth through education, charity, and civic engagement.

  • Inclusive community events
  • Volunteer & charity drives
  • Youth mentorship & scholarships
  • Interfaith & intercultural dialogue
